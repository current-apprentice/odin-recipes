Tacos Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of wide pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and a savory filling of ground meat (such as beef or sausage), spinach, and cheese, baked until golden and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or sausage with diced onion and minced garlic until browned and cooked through. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a baking dish.
  4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce on the bottom of the baking dish, then layer with cooked lasagna noodles.
  5. Spread a layer of ricotta cheese over the noodles, followed by a layer of cooked meat mixture and a layer of spinach.
  6. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, then repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of noodles on top.
  7. Optionally, pour béchamel sauce over the top layer of noodles.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for about 25-30 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
