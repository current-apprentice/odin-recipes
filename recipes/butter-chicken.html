Butter Chicken recipe

Description

Butter chicken is a classic Indian dish known for its rich and creamy tomato-based sauce, tender pieces of marinated chicken, and fragrant spices.

This beloved dish strikes a perfect balance between savory and mildly sweet flavors, making it a favorite in Indian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Chicken(boneles,skkinless)
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Garam masala
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Butter
  • Tomato puree
  • Heavy cream
  • Kasuri methi(dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt, lemon juice, ginger garlic paste, garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to an hour.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until it's browned on all sides and cooked through. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add more butter if needed and saut√© tomato puree until it's cooked down and thickened.
  4. Add heavy cream and kasuri methi to the tomato mixture, stirring well to combine.
  5. Add the cooked chicken back into the sauce and simmer for a few minutes until everything is heated through and well combined.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary, adding sugar if desired for a slightly sweeter flavor.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Enjoy your delicious butter chicken with naan or rice!
